Adopting

Minnesota Waiting Child Adoption

There are currently 355 children under State guardianship in immediate need of families.

HOPE works with the Minnesota Public Private Adoption Initiative (PPAI), a state funded program, and the Wendy’s Wonderful Kids Program to bring Minnesota children in the foster care system into permanent families.

All of these Minnesota Waiting Children have special needs, whether behavioral, emotional or physical. These children are over the age of 6. Many are a part of sibling groups and most are children of color.

Costs

Parents who adopt through the Minnesota PPAI program pay little to no fees. Both Minnesota and Wisconsin families are eligible. Learn more by filling out this Online Request form or attending a PPAI Orientation meeting Training Link.
